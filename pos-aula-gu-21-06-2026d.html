Pós-aula — Food & Restaurants

21 de junho de 2026 · Science English

Resumo da aula

Today we practiced ordering food and talking about restaurants in English. You learned useful phrases for when you eat out or visit an English-speaking country.

Vocabulário

Appetizer — entrada / aperitivo
"Can we start with some appetizers?"
Main course — prato principal
"I'll have the grilled salmon as my main course."
Bill / Check — conta
"Could we have the bill, please?"
Tip — gorjeta
"It's common to leave a 15% tip in the US."

Frase do dia

"I'd like to make a reservation for two." — Gostaria de fazer uma reserva para dois.

Para praticar

Tente montar um diálogo em inglês pedindo comida em um restaurante. Use as frases que aprendemos hoje!
