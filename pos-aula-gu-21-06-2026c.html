Pós-aula — At the Gym

21 de junho de 2026 · Science English

Resumo da aula

Today we practiced vocabulary related to the gym and physical activities. You learned how to talk about your workout routine and describe exercises in English.

Vocabulário

Work out — malhar / treinar
"I work out three times a week."
Warm up — aquecer
"Always warm up before you start lifting."
Cool down — desaquecer / alongar
"Don't forget to cool down after your session."
Personal trainer — personal trainer
"My personal trainer gave me a new routine."

Frase do dia

"I've been hitting the gym regularly." — Tenho ido à academia regularmente.

Para praticar

Descreva seu treino em inglês! Que exercícios você faz? Com que frequência você vai à academia?
