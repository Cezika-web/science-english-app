Pós-aula — Daily Routines

21 de junho de 2026 · Science English

Resumo da aula

Today we talked about daily routines and how to describe your day in English. You learned how to use the simple present tense to talk about habits and regular activities.

Vocabulário

Wake up — acordar
"I wake up at 7am every day."
Get ready — se preparar
"She gets ready for work in the morning."
Have breakfast — tomar café da manhã
"We have breakfast together on Sundays."
Commute — deslocar para o trabalho/escola
"He commutes by bus every day."

Expressão do dia

"What's your daily routine like?" — Como é a sua rotina diária?
